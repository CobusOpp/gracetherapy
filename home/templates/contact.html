Covid-19 safety

  • To ensure your safety and that of our staff, hand sanitizer is provided at the door. Please sanitize as you enter.
  • Please wear your mask at all times when entering the building and the therapy rooms.
  • During the pandemic, a child may only be accompanied by one parent please.
  • Please forgive us if we don’t shake hands. We may however give you a friendly wave, or elbow bump on request.
  • Surfaces such as computer keyboards, books, toys and therapy equipment are sanitized regularly.
  • At Grace Therapy we are doing all we can to stay healthy so that we can continue to provide you with the individual attention your child deserves.
  • Thank you for your support in maintaining a healthy environment for everyone.
